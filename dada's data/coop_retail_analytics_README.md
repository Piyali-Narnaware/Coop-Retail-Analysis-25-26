# Co-op Convenience Retail Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyses a synthetic convenience retail dataset inspired by Co-op store operations. The dashboard is designed as a Power BI portfolio project for retail, customer insight, CRM analytics, and commercial analyst roles.

The objective is to help store and commercial teams understand revenue performance, membership contribution, category trends, and operational availability risks across stores.

## Dataset

The dataset contains 5,000 transaction-level records with the following fields:

- `Date`
- `Store`
- `Category`
- `Customer_ID`
- `Revenue`
- `Units`
- `Member`
- `Availability_Pct`

## Business Questions

1. Which stores and categories are driving revenue?
2. How much revenue is coming from Co-op members versus non-members?
3. Are member baskets stronger than non-member baskets?
4. Which stores are at risk due to lower product availability?
5. How is revenue trending over time?

## Key Insights From the Dataset

- Total revenue across the dataset is approximately **£199,303**.
- The highest revenue store is **Croydon**, generating approximately **£36,157**.
- The leading category is **Own Brand**, generating approximately **£27,024**.
- Average basket value is **£39.86**.
- Average product availability is **95.0%**.
- Member revenue share is **54.9%**.
- The lowest availability store is **London Central**, with average availability of **95.0%**.

## Recommended Business Actions

1. **Prioritise availability recovery in lower-performing stores.**  
   Stores below the 95% availability target should be reviewed for replenishment gaps, stock ordering issues, or demand forecasting misses.

2. **Use category-level performance to optimise ranging and promotions.**  
   High-revenue categories should receive stronger promotional placement, while weaker categories should be reviewed for pricing, availability, and local demand.

3. **Strengthen member conversion strategy.**  
   If members show stronger basket value or revenue contribution, stores can use targeted offers to convert more non-members into members.

4. **Monitor store performance through monthly trading rhythm.**  
   A monthly revenue trend helps managers identify seasonal changes, local store issues, and campaign impact.
